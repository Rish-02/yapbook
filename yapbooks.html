YAP Books

Ice Creams and Time Machines

What happens when a writer turns mentor?

An anthology is born! Ice Creams and Time Machines is a Young Author Program (YAP) anthology. Neelima coaches budding young writers on how to create a plot and characters. The stories that emerge from her workshop are a testament to the depth of a child’s creativity if they are simply nudged in the right direction. The book was the result of several workshops conducted by Neelima across Bangalore.

Footprints

Vampires at a blood cream parlor!

A koala in a WWII setting, an enterprising young lady who has to make important career choices, magical giraffes, a Pharoah’s dilemma, wizards on other planets, death walkers in alternate universes, dogs and penguins who go the moon, health warriors battling the pandemic, twins who show their strong resolve…these are just some of the exciting ideas that children have come up with at the Young Author Program (YAP) sessions conducted by Neelima.

The Firebirds Sing

A book full of angels, tooth fairies, vampires, samurais, and dinosaurs!

The Firebirds Sing is a Young Author Program (YAP) anthology – the third one of the series. The book is the result of a series of YAP workshops hosted in 2021. Open the book and fall into the fantastical world of angels, firebirds, tooth fairies, spies, vampires, samurais, and dinosaur-like Arganosago! The young authors who are featured in this book are well-aware of realistic themes as well, including the migrant crisis, old age, and the plight of animals as well as the lyrical drama of Shakespearean verse.

Griffins and Gabbar Singh

A whole bunch of information.

Griffins and Gabbar Singh is a Young Author Program (YAP) anthology – the fourth one of the series. The book is the result of a series of workshops hosted in 2021. This book is filled with delicious plots and traverses the deserts of Rajasthan, alien planets, outer space, schools, flying academies, and forests. The characters are kind, determined, rebellious, clever, and even deadly! Young writers are an ambitious lot and this book goes to show how there are no barriers to the creativity of young minds.
